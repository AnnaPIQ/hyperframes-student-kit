## Beats

**1 · Hook (TH 0–8.58)** — Open on the founder. Logo wipes in top-left, thin flame rule under it. VO: *"…biggest revenue month ever but nothing left in the bank…"* Quick fade-up, no caption.

**2 · Looks-good (GFX 8.58–18.68)** — Three stat tiles stagger up — **Revenue ↑**, **Orders ↑**, **Growth ↑** — glowing blue-tint, climbing counters, faint green up-arrows. Everything *looks* great. Eases: power3.out (rise), back.out (tiles), sine (counter). Exit: tiles dim.

**3 · The turn (TH 18.68–25.10)** — Cut to founder. VO: *"…the cash never sticks around. Here's what most founders miss."* The human pivot.

**4 · Revenue → Keep (GFX 25.10–36.86)** — Big **REVENUE** wordmark celebrated (sky glow, scale-in), then a flame hairline cracks through it; resolves to the question **"How much did you _keep_?"** — *keep* in Hedvig serif italic flame. Eases: expo.out, power4.in (crack), back.out.

**5 · Costs grow too (TH 36.86–40.42)** — Short founder beat. VO: *"…costs have a habit of growing with them."*

**6 · The profit problem (GFX 40.42–55.18)** — Phase A: five cost rows climb in, staggered, with rising bars in flame — **Ad spend · Shipping · Returns · COGS · Discounts**. Phase B: rows collapse into two bars — **Revenue** tall & climbing (blue) vs **Profit** flat (flame outline). VO: *"…revenue setting records while profit stands still."* Centerpiece data-viz.

**7 · Dryft Sleep (TH 55.18–62.98)** — Founder + a small "Dryft Sleep · client" chip. VO: *"…brands like Dryft Sleep focus on numbers that drive profitability, not just top-line growth."*

**8 · Partner (GFX 62.98–71.50)** — **EcomIQ × Dryft Sleep** lockup; line: *metrics that matter — long after the first purchase.* Quiet, trust-building. Eases: power2.out, sine.

**9 · Payoff (GFX 71.50–78.92)** — Thesis: **"Revenue is what you sold."** (struck through) → **"Profit is what you _keep_."** → a single coin drops into a bank-account glyph. VO: *"…only one of them ends up in your bank account."*

**10 · Direct (TH 78.92–86.18)** — Founder, direct address. VO: *"…we can help you find out why."*

**11 · CTA (GFX 86.18–90.50)** — Flame CTA card: **"Find out why your profit isn't showing up."** + flame pill **"Click the link below →"** + EcomIQ logo. Hold ~2s past VO.

## Structure (rule of threes)
- **Hook** (0–18.7s, ~21%): record revenue, but no cash.
- **Body** (18.7–71.5s, ~58%): why — revenue hides problems, costs creep, profit stalls, Dryft Sleep proof.
- **Payoff + CTA** (71.5–90.5s, ~21%): profit is what you keep → click the link.
