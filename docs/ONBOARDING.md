# Make a Video Ad — Getting Started (no experience needed)

You don't need to know anything technical. You'll click two email invites, set a
password, open a project, and **type what you want** — like texting a video editor.

---

## Part A — For the owner (Anna): 3 quick setup steps

Do these once per new person. After this, send them **Part B**.

1. **Invite them to the project (GitHub).**
   GitHub is just where the project is stored. On github.com → open
   `AnnaPIQ/hyperframes-student-kit` → **Settings → Collaborators → Add people** →
   type **their email** → send. GitHub emails them an invite and walks them through
   making a free account.
2. **Give them Claude access.**
   They need a Claude account that includes Claude Code. Easiest: add them to your
   **Claude Team** (Team settings → invite by email) so it's on your plan. Otherwise
   they sign up for a paid Claude plan themselves.
3. **Send them Part B** (below) and tell them which email to watch for.

That's it — everything else (the studio, the brand, the ad tools) is already built
into the project and sets itself up automatically.

---

## Part B — For you (the new person): your step-by-step

### Step 1 — Accept the project invite
You'll get an email inviting you to **hyperframes-student-kit**. Open it and click
the **Accept / Join** button. If it asks you to create a free account, just use your
email and pick a password — takes a minute. *(This is only to let you into the
project. You'll never have to think about it again.)*

### Step 2 — Open Claude
Go to **https://claude.ai/code** in your web browser and sign in (use the invite or
login the owner set up for you).

### Step 3 — Open the project
You'll see a list of projects. Click **hyperframes-student-kit**.

### Step 4 — Wait about a minute
Some text will scroll by while it gets the studio ready. **You can ignore all of it.**
When it settles down, you're good to go.

### Step 5 — Tell it what you want
At the bottom there's a message box. Type this and press enter:

> **help me make an ad**

### Step 6 — Answer its friendly questions
It'll ask a few simple things, one at a time:
- What's the ad about, and what do you want people to do?
- Where will it run? *(Instagram feed · Reels/Stories/TikTok · square · YouTube/website)*
- Do you have a product photo? *(If yes, drag it into the message box. If no, that's fine.)*

Just answer in plain words. There are no wrong answers.

### Step 7 — Look at the first version
It builds your ad and **shows you a video right in the chat.** Watch it, then either:
- Ask for changes in plain English — *"make the headline bigger"*, *"use a brighter
  orange"*, *"swap the photo"*, or
- Say **"looks great, finish it!"**

### Step 8 — Get your finished ad
It makes the final, high-quality version and **sends you the video to download.**
Done — that's your ad. 🎬

---

## Handy things to know
- **Talk to it like a person.** "Make a punchy Instagram ad for our summer sale, 20%
  off, button says Shop Now." It takes it from there.
- **Your video clip is too big to drag in?** Put it in Google Drive, set it to "anyone
  with the link," and paste the link in the chat.
- **Want another version?** Just say so — *"make a square one too"* or *"try a
  different headline."*
- **Stuck or confused?** Type *"I'm stuck, can you explain in simple terms?"* — it'll
  help. You can't break anything.

**The only thing to remember:** open the project, and type **"help me make an ad."**
