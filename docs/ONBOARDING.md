# Make a Video Ad — Getting Started (no experience needed)

You don't need to know anything technical. You'll make one free account, click an
invite, open a project, and **type what you want** — like texting a video editor.

---

## Part A — For the owner (Anna): 2 quick setup steps

Do these once per new person, then send them **Part B**.

1. **Invite them to the project (GitHub).**
   GitHub is just where the project files live. On github.com → open
   `AnnaPIQ/hyperframes-student-kit` → **Settings → Collaborators → Add people** →
   type **their email** → send.
2. **Make sure they can use Claude Code.**
   Claude Code is included with a paid Claude plan. If they already have one, they're
   set. If not, add them to your **Claude Team** (Team settings → invite by email).

Everything else — the studio, the brand, the ad tools — is already built into the
project and sets itself up automatically.

---

## Part B — For you (the new person): your step-by-step

### First, the two things this uses (you barely touch either)
- **GitHub** — just where the project is stored. You make one free account, click an
  invite, and then **never have to think about it again.**
- **Claude Code** — this is **Claude with your video studio attached.** It lives at
  **claude.ai/code** (note the **`/code`** on the end). It is *not* the normal Claude
  chat — it's the **same Claude login**, just a different page where the project and
  the ad-making tools live. The regular Claude chat can talk about ads; **Claude Code
  actually builds them.**

### Step 1 — Create your free GitHub account
1. Open a web browser and go to **https://github.com**
2. Click **Sign up** (top right).
3. Type your **email**, make up a **password**, and pick any **username**.
4. GitHub emails you a code — type it in to confirm. Done. It's free, and that's all
   the "GitHub" you'll ever need to do.

### Step 2 — Accept the project invite
Check your email for a message from **GitHub** inviting you to
**hyperframes-student-kit**. Open it and click **Accept invitation**. *(That's the
last time GitHub matters.)*

### Step 3 — Open Claude Code
Go to **https://claude.ai/code** and sign in with your normal Claude login.
⚠️ Make sure it's **claude.ai/code** — with `/code` on the end. (Plain claude.ai is
the regular chat and can't make videos.)

### Step 4 — Open the project
You'll see a list of projects. Click **hyperframes-student-kit**.

### Step 5 — Wait about a minute
Some text will scroll by while it gets the studio ready. **Ignore all of it.** When it
settles down, you're ready.

### Step 6 — Tell it what you want
At the bottom there's a message box. Type this and press enter:

> **help me make an ad**

### Step 7 — Answer its friendly questions
It'll ask a few simple things, one at a time:
- What's the ad about, and what do you want people to do?
- Where will it run? *(Instagram feed · Reels/Stories/TikTok · square · YouTube/website)*
- Do you have a product photo? *(If yes, drag it into the message box. If no, that's fine.)*

Answer in plain words. There are no wrong answers.

### Step 8 — Look at the first version
It builds your ad and **shows you a video right in the chat.** Watch it, then either:
- Ask for changes in plain English — *"make the headline bigger"*, *"use a brighter
  orange"*, *"swap the photo"*, or
- Say **"looks great, finish it!"**

### Step 9 — Get your finished ad
It makes the final, high-quality version and **sends you the video to download.**
Done — that's your ad. 🎬

## Handy things to know
- **Talk to it like a person.** "Make a punchy Instagram ad for our summer sale, 20%
  off, button says Shop Now." It takes it from there.
- **Your video clip is too big to drag in?** Put it in Google Drive, set it to "anyone
  with the link," and paste the link in the chat.
- **Want another version?** Just say so — *"make a square one too"* or *"try a
  different headline."*
- **Stuck or confused?** Type *"I'm stuck, can you explain in simple terms?"* — you
  can't break anything.

**The only thing to remember:** open the project at **claude.ai/code**, and type
**"help me make an ad."**
